Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SHARK TECH, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications available on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information in those apps.

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Our apps do not require an account, do not ask for your name, email address, or any identifying information, and do not send any user data to our servers — because we don't have any servers that receive user data.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

All data you enter into our apps (such as tracked dates or preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device except as described below.

3. Network Access

Our apps may connect to the internet solely to fetch publicly available information required for app functionality, such as:

  • USCIS Visa Bulletin data (published at uscis.gov — public information)
  • App configuration data hosted on GitHub (public data)

No personal data is transmitted during these requests. The requests are equivalent to visiting a public webpage.

4. Notifications

Push notifications in our apps are generated and delivered locally on your device. We do not use third-party push notification services that transmit data to remote servers.

5. In-App Purchases

Some apps offer optional one-time purchases through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not receive or store your payment information. Google's Privacy Policy governs all billing transactions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics services, or tracking libraries. The only third-party service involved is Google Play Billing for in-app purchases, which is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

8. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. All data stored by our apps lives on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contactus@gosharktech.com. We will respond within 30 days.
