Privacy Policy

SHARK TECH, LLC — gosharktech.com

Last updated: May 2026

SHARK TECH, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications available on the Google Play Store, including any of our immigration tracking and test preparation apps. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information in those apps and on this website.

1. No Personal Data Collected or Stored

We do not collect, retain, or store any personal information on our servers. Our apps do not require an account, do not ask for your name or email address, and do not store any user data on our servers. Case numbers transmitted to retrieve status information are used solely to query government systems and are never logged, stored, or retained on our end.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

All data you enter into our apps (such as tracked priority dates or preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device except as described in Section 3 below. You can remove all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

3. Network Access

Our apps connect to the internet solely to retrieve information required for app functionality. For case status apps, this works as follows:

  • My Case Tracker / My Receipt Decoder: Your receipt number is sent to our secure backend server (hosted on Google Cloud Run), which queries the USCIS Case Status API on your behalf and returns the result. Your receipt number is used only to make this query — it is never stored, logged, or retained on our server.
  • My Visa Priority Alert: The app fetches Visa Bulletin data from publicly available JSON files hosted on GitHub Gist (gist.githubusercontent.com/gosharktech), maintained by SHARK TECH, LLC. No personal data is transmitted.
  • My NVC Case Tracker: The app queries the CEAC case status system at ceac.state.gov. No personal data beyond the case number is transmitted, and it is not stored by us.
  • My Citizenship Prep: Civics test content is bundled with the app. No network access is required for core functionality.
  • App configuration data hosted on GitHub (public data)

We operate a backend server solely as a secure proxy to government APIs. This server does not store, log, or retain case numbers or any user data. It exists to keep government API credentials secure, as required by those APIs' terms of service.

4. Notifications

Push notifications in our apps are generated and delivered locally on your device using Android's built-in notification system. We do not use third-party push notification services, and no data is transmitted to remote servers in order to deliver notifications.

5. In-App Purchases

Some apps offer optional one-time purchases through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information at any point. Google's Privacy Policy governs all billing transactions. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. No Advertising or Tracking

Our apps do not include advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, crash reporting services, or any other third-party tracking tools. We do not serve ads. We do not track your usage. We do not sell or share data with any third party for marketing purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at contactus@gosharktech.com and we will address it promptly.

8. This Website

This website (gosharktech.com) is a static informational site. It does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts of any kind. No information is collected when you visit this site.

9. Your Rights

We do not store personal data on our servers. Case numbers transit through our backend server solely to query government APIs and are never retained. Any data stored by our apps exists only on your device and can be permanently removed at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its local storage. If you contact us requesting data deletion, we will confirm that no personal data is stored by us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: contactus@gosharktech.com
  • Website: gosharktech.com

We will respond within 30 days.
